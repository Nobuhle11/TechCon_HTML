Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for software developers. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most influential tech conferences worldwide, fostering innovation, collaboration, and knowledge-sharing.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower tech professionals by providing a platform for learning, networking, and showcasing cutting-edge innovations that shape the future of technology.

Past Speakers

We’ve had the honor of hosting some of the most brilliant minds in tech:

  • Ada Lovelace – A visionary speaker who explored the foundations of computing and AI ethics.
  • Alan Turing – Known for his groundbreaking work in cryptography and artificial intelligence.
  • Grace Hopper – Celebrated for her innovation in programming languages and leadership in computer science.
